M-Ward Community Led Action and Partnership

About the Project

CLAP proposes a community led approach that addresses the vulnerabilities of people in informal settlements, educates them to move away from the fear and stigma surrounding COVID and empowers them to cope with it through an effective primary level redressal, thereby reducing the mortality among the positive cases is thus considered necessary. Piloting such a model in a large informal settlement in Mumbai will be a learning site for other cities with informal settlement populations.
