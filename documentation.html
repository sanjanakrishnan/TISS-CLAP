M-WARD CLAP Project: Documentation

Overview

Community Led Action and Partnership (CLAP) project started work in August 2020, in M-Ward, Mumbai, during the first wave of the Corona virus pandemic. The project centres around a community led approach to addresses the vulnerabilities of people in informal settlements, and empowers them to cope with disasters through an effective primary level redressal. Piloting such a model in a large informal settlement in Mumbai will be a learning site for other cities with informal settlement populations.

This webpage documents the process and the data collated and created by the CLAP Project from August 2020 to May 2021. It contains links to the survey data and several sub-projects including ration card, relief activities, vulnerability index etc.

Structure

This is an archive of the CLAP data as of May 2021.

  1. Project outputs: Contains the important documented outputs of the project, including the project report, final presentation, report to donors etc.
  2. Survey data aggregation, coding and cleaning: Project survey data of 25579 households and 97414 individuals for 194 qanuestions. Additionally contains the raw data from survey, coding of the survey and documenting cleaning codes used to create the final dataset.
  3. Findings from survey on individuals and households: Contains the findings from the survey across various parameters for the 26 bastis. M-Ward level, basti-wise and basti comparision covered
  4. Basti-level data: Data collected on the 26 bastis on macro-indicators about the basti. This includes the sanitation survey and data on critical indicators like the status of basti in the developement plan, land ownership, prevalance of communicable and non-communucable diseases etc.
  5. Focussed Group Discussions: this link contains all the data of FGDs conducted during the project. It can be used to understand the impact of first wave of COVID on citizens of M-East ward.
  6. Schemes database, matching and letter to households: A database of 131 active and updated schemes in Maharastra was created, along with the documents required to apply for them. This database was used to identify the schemes that each individual is eligible for. Further, a customized letter was sent to each household informing them about key contact numbers and scheme ekigibility.
  7. CLAP Maps: Contains the maps created as part of this project to mark slum boudaries, geocode households etc. Also containes scanned copies of all maps marked with HHIDs created by CRFs for all communities
  8. Vulnerability Index: The vulnerability index aims to assign a vulnerability score to each individual, household and basti. This score aims to capture different aspects of vulnerability arrising from the lack of health, essential services,
  9. Ration project: aims to fix issues in ration distribution system.
  10. Relief operations: started during the second lockdown in Maharashtra, this contains the ration requests, delivered, and community volunteers in the project
  11. Vaccination drive: CLAP started a vaccine awareness and registration drive in the 26 bastis, and further provided transportation to and from the vaccine centres. This enabled the vaccination of 500 families as of 13th May
