Datasets

Relief distribution was started during the second state-wide lockdown in Maharashtra in April 2021. A google form was created, it was used by CLAP volunteers to fill details of the volunteers. Further, iCall through its helpline was maintaining a sheet to record ration requests.
Volunteers and dedicated volunteers used the “Search” app to input the HHID of those requesting ration when available.

Feeding from Far (FFF) was the main food delivery partner for CLAP. As kits were limited, the vulnerability scores were used to prioritize vaccines. The HHID and index was added (hh_index- average of individuals and households). These were used to prioritize the vaccines (70-30 formula). A separate line was maintained for iCall requests.
The data was presented in a dashboard for transparancy and easy access to information.

Other outputs

ration distribution rationale + communication material

As the number of ration kits were limited, they were targetted to those who need it to the most. 60-70% of the ration was decided by the households and individuals vulnerability index. 30-40% was given to those outside the survey and prioritized for those who did not have ration cards. Further, women-led households, widows and the requests through iCall were prioritized.
Communication material was created that could be used by CLAP volunteers to explain this rationale for ration distribution.

Relief application and dashboard

Application was created to display the data collected transparantly and provide regular updates on the status of ration distribution to those involved in relief work. The application has two functionalities:

  • Dashboard: to display the daily requests and distribution of ration and food packets, over time and basti wise

  • Search table: to search and see details of all individual requests that have come in, if they have been addressed and when.
